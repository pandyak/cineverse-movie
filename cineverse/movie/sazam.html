Description: Shazam! (2019) tells the story of 14-year-old Billy Batson, a foster child who is chosen by an ancient wizard to become the superhero Shazam. By shouting the magic word “Shazam,” Billy transforms into an adult superhero with extraordinary powers. While trying to have fun with his new abilities, Billy must face the evil Dr. Thaddeus Sivana, who seeks the powers for himself. This lighthearted DC movie blends humor, action, and heartwarming family themes, making it one of the most entertaining superhero films in recent years.
