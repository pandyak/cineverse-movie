Iron Man 3

Duration: 2h 10m
Director: Shane Black

⭐ Rating: 7.1 / 10

Description: Tony Stark faces his most powerful enemy yet — the Mandarin — while grappling with the trauma of the events in New York (The Avengers). When his world is torn apart by terrorists, Stark embarks on a journey of survival and discovery, relying on his intelligence and ingenuity without his full arsenal of suits.

Alongside Pepper Potts and James Rhodes, Stark must uncover a dangerous conspiracy involving the Extremis virus. The film combines high-stakes action with Tony’s personal struggle, testing what defines the man inside the armor.

Iron Man 3 explores themes of identity, resilience, and heroism, while delivering thrilling battles, emotional depth, and a fresh perspective on the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
