Doctor Strange

Release Date: November 4, 2016
Duration: 1h 55m
Director: Scott Derrickson
Producer: Kevin Feige

⭐ Rating: 7.5 / 10

Description: Doctor Stephen Strange, a world-renowned neurosurgeon, suffers a devastating car accident that destroys his hands, ending his medical career. Desperate for healing, he journeys to Kamar-Taj, where he discovers the hidden world of mysticism and alternate dimensions. Under the guidance of the Ancient One, Strange trains to become a master of the mystical arts, learning spells, portals, and powerful relics such as the Eye of Agamotto.

As he grows in power, Strange faces dangerous adversaries, including Kaecilius and Dormammu, who threaten the balance of reality. Through mind-bending magical battles, alternate timelines, and reality-warping visuals, Strange embraces his role as the Sorcerer Supreme, protector of Earth from mystical and interdimensional threats.
