Black Panther

Release Date: February 16, 2018
Duration: 2h 14m
Director: Ryan Coogler
Producer: Kevin Feige

⭐ Rating: 7.3 / 10

Description: After the death of his father, T’Challa returns home to the advanced African nation of Wakanda to take his rightful place as king. However, a powerful enemy, Erik Killmonger, challenges him for the throne, forcing T’Challa to embrace the mantle of Black Panther and defend his people.

The film showcases Wakanda’s advanced technology, rich culture, and the responsibilities of leadership. T’Challa must balance being a superhero and a king, while facing internal and external threats to his nation. Alongside strong allies like Nakia, Okoye, and Shuri, he navigates complex political and personal challenges.

Black Panther explores themes of legacy, identity, responsibility, and social justice, while delivering high-octane action, stunning visuals, and emotional depth. It’s a landmark Marvel film that highlights representation, culture, and heroism on a global scale.
