Description: Death Note, based on the manga by Tsugumi Ohba and Takeshi Obata, follows Light Yagami, a brilliant high school student who discovers a supernatural notebook that allows him to kill anyone by writing their name while visualizing their face. Using the Death Note, Light seeks to create a "new world" free of crime, but his actions draw the attention of L, a genius detective determined to stop him. Known for its intense psychological battles, moral dilemmas, and intricate plotting, Death Note is a gripping thriller that explores power and corruption.

Notable Death Note Rules

Rule Description Image
Basic Killing Rule The human whose name is written in the Death Note shall die, provided the writer visualizes their face.
Time of Death Death occurs within 40 seconds of writing if no cause is specified; otherwise, details must be written within 6 minutes and 40 seconds.
Shinigami Eyes A human can trade half their lifespan for the ability to see others’ names and lifespans, aiding precise kills.
Ownership Rule The Death Note’s user must keep it in their possession; relinquishing it erases memories of its use unless reclaimed.
