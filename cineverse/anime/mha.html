My Hero Academia

Release Date: April 3, 2016
Duration: 24–25m per episode
Creator: Kohei Horikoshi
Producers: Bones, Toho Animation
Total Episodes: 159
Total Season: 7

⭐ Rating: 8.5 / 10

Description: My Hero Academia, based on Kohei Horikoshi’s manga, is set in a world where almost everyone has superpowers, known as Quirks. Izuku Midoriya, a Quirkless boy, inherits the powers of the world’s greatest hero, All Might, and enrolls in U.A. High School to train as a professional hero. Alongside his classmates, Midoriya faces villains, personal challenges, and the responsibility of becoming a symbol of peace. Known for its dynamic action, emotional depth, and inspiring themes, My Hero Academia is a modern shonen masterpiece.
