Description: Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a Japanese anime based on Koyoharu Gotouge’s manga. Set in Taisho-era Japan, it follows Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons, and his sister Nezuko is turned into one. Joining the Demon Slayer Corps, Tanjiro trains in Water Breathing techniques to hunt demons and protect humanity while seeking a cure for Nezuko. Known for its stunning animation by Ufotable, emotional storytelling, and intense battles, the series has become a global phenomenon.
