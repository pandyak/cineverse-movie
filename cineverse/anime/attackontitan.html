Description: Attack on Titan, based on Hajime Isayama’s manga, is set in a post-apocalyptic world where humanity lives behind walls to protect against Titans, giant humanoid creatures that devour humans. The story follows Eren Yeager, Mikasa Ackerman, and Armin Arlert as they join the Survey Corps to fight Titans and uncover the secrets of their world. Known for its intense action, complex characters, and shocking plot twists, the series blends dark fantasy with political intrigue and philosophical themes, earning global acclaim for its storytelling and animation by Wit Studio and MAPPA.
