Description: The Boys is a gritty superhero series set in a world where superheroes, known as "Supes," are celebrities managed by the powerful corporation Vought International. Based on the comic by Garth Ennis and Darick Robertson, the show follows a vigilante group, led by Billy Butcher, as they take on corrupt and dangerous Supes, including the narcissistic Homelander. Packed with dark humor, intense action, and social commentary, the series explores themes of power, corruption, and morality, earning praise for its bold storytelling and compelling ensemble cast.
