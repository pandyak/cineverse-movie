Description: Money Heist (La Casa de Papel) is a Spanish crime thriller series that follows a criminal mastermind known as "The Professor" who plans the biggest heist in history: robbing the Royal Mint of Spain. Recruiting a team of eight criminals with city-based codenames, the group takes hostages and executes a meticulously planned operation filled with twists, betrayals, and high-stakes drama. The series, set in Madrid, is renowned for its gripping narrative, complex characters, and themes of resistance, earning global acclaim and a massive fanbase.
