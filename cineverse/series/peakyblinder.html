Description: Peaky Blinders is a British crime drama set in Birmingham, England, in the aftermath of World War I. The series follows the Shelby family, led by Thomas "Tommy" Shelby, a cunning and ambitious gangster who runs the Peaky Blinders gang. Known for their signature flat caps with razor blades sewn into the brims, the gang navigates the criminal underworld, political intrigue, and personal struggles. The show is celebrated for its gritty storytelling, stylish cinematography, and complex characters, blending historical events with intense drama.
